# 🧪 Gas-Liquid Absorption Column Simulator

[![MATLAB](https://img.shields.io/badge/Made%20with-MATLAB-orange)](https://www.mathworks.com/)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-blue.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

This application, developed in **MATLAB App Designer**, is a powerful academic tool designed to model and simulate the behavior of a gas-liquid absorption column. It enables mass balance analysis, experimental titration processing, and performance evaluation for mass transfer processes.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Dynamic Configuration:** Interactive control over temperature, flow rates, pressure, and packing height.
* **Titration Processing:** Automated calculation of concentrations and moles absorbed from experimental lab data.
* **Mass Balance:** Visual representation of inlet and outlet streams (Gas/Liquid) with molar fractions.
* **Performance Analysis:** Dynamic plots showing absorption efficiency and $CO_2$ moles absorbed over time.
* **Export Capability:** Integrated one-click export to Excel for technical reporting.

## 🛠️ How to Use

1. **Requirements:** MATLAB with *App Designer* installed.
2. **Execution:**
   - Open the `.mlapp` file in MATLAB.
   - Click the **Run** button in the App Designer toolbar.
3. **Workflow:**
   - Input your operating parameters in the *Teóricos y Experimentales* tab.
   - Enter your titration data and click **Procesar titulación**.
   - View the results and auto-generated plots in the *Resultados* and *Flujos* tabs.
   - Click **Exportar a Excel** to generate your data report.
